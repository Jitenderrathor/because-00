Challenge
The current branding of Lodestone faces challenges such as weak online presence, lack of storytelling, and information. The rebranding aims to embody Lodestone's values concisely, becoming a seamless part of its brand experience. The new design had to be a manifestation of the values defining the identity structure and reflect the proposed brand language. It had to be concise, conveying the most interesting way possible. The target audience varies from high ranking officials (CMOs, CTOs) to entrepreneurs looking for Quality testing services.
Deliverables
  • Logo Design
  • Rebranding
  • Collaterals
  • Design Mockups
  • Content Strategy
  • Iconography
  • Development Assets
Approach
Branding is not a one time exercise, it is always a continuous process. Thus, while creating the brand identity, the approach was to always think of the brand holistically and to keep the overall picture in mind, including the brand extension. The journey was strictly authentic experience where the brand speaks not through advertisements and brochures but rather, through visual manifestation of its uniqueness.
