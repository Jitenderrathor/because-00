Establishing a Comprehensive Digital Storefront for Tiles Center
Tiles.Center, a US-based tile distributor, offers a wide range of top-quality ceramic, vitrified, and porcelain tiles, catering to diverse sectors with large-size porcelain slabs that ensure every project phase exceeds expectations.
Challenge
Tiles Center approached Because with a specific need: to create an effective online platform that showcases their extensive product range, compensates for the absence of high-quality product images, and simplifies navigation through their vast inventory.
Approach
Because tackled these challenges by analyzing the U.S. market to align the website with customer preferences. We utilized AI to generate lifestyle images and developed the website on an e-commerce platform for its flexibility, ensuring easy usage, management, and scalability. The focus was on a user-friendly design to facilitate effortless navigation.
